Although they take place in our luxury boutique studios, body transformations are a science. With the right knowledge, the best exercise equipment, comfortable surroundings, and elite-level wrap-around support, the hurdles that stand between you and the body you want are removed.

Welcome to Fitness Lab. We’re an elite personal training service with a difference. Trust us with your health and fitness goals, and we’ll help you achieve results beyond what most people think is possible.

Why Choose Us

We do things differently

The moment you step into a Fitness Lab personal training gym, you’ll recognise that we have a different approach. Here, your personal training sessions and workouts are private – held in a fully-equipped pod with everything you need immediately to hand.

Your dedicated expert personal trainer understands you, the level you’re working at, your goals, and how to help you push your exercise to the next level – whether you’ve got specific fitness and weight loss goals or simply want to feel stronger and healthier.

Whether you’re just starting out or you’re a professional athlete, our personal training service will make sure you have everything you need to achieve whatever kind of body transformation you’re looking for.
